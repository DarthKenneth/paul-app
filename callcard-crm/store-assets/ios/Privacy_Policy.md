# Privacy Policy — Rolodeck

**Effective Date:** April 3, 2026
**App:** Rolodeck
**Developer:** ArdinGate Studios LLC
**Contact:** privacy@ardingate.com

---

## Summary

Rolodeck stores all data **locally on your device only**. We do not collect, transmit,
or have access to any of your customer data. No account is required.

---

## Data We Collect

Rolodeck stores the following data exclusively in your device's local storage
(via AsyncStorage, a key-value store on your device):

| Data Type | What's Stored | Why |
|-----------|--------------|-----|
| Customer records | Name, email, phone, address, zip code | Core app functionality |
| Service log entries | Date, type (service/install), notes | Core app functionality |
| App settings | Selected color theme, sort preference | Persisting your preferences |
| Square Access Token | Your Square API key (if you choose to add it) | Optional future invoice feature |

**None of this data is transmitted to ArdinGate Studios LLC or any third party.**
**We have no servers that receive, store, or process your customer data.**

---

## Third-Party Services

### Square (Optional Integration)

If you choose to use the Square invoice integration (a future feature), you will
enter your own Square merchant access token in the Settings screen. This token
is stored locally on your device only.

When the Square integration is active, Rolodeck will make API requests directly
from your device to Square's servers using your token. ArdinGate Studios LLC does
not proxy, intercept, or store these requests or your token on our servers.

Square's handling of your data is governed by Square's own Privacy Policy:
https://squareup.com/us/en/legal/general/privacy

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored in your device's local storage. You can delete all app data
by deleting the Rolodeck app from your device. Uninstalling the app removes all
stored customer records, service entries, settings, and your Square access token.

There is no server-side data to delete because we never receive any.

---

## Data Security

We recommend securing your device with a passcode or biometric authentication
to protect the customer information stored in Rolodeck. The app itself does not
implement additional encryption beyond what iOS/Android provides at the OS level.

Your Square access token, if entered, is stored via AsyncStorage. We recommend
using a Personal Access Token scoped with only the permissions required
(INVOICES_WRITE, CUSTOMERS_READ) rather than your full Square account credentials.
