# What's New — v2.0.0 (App Store)

Paste this into App Store Connect → Version → "What's New in This Version".
Apple max is 4000 chars; this draft is ~1100.

---

Cross-device sync just got a major upgrade.

Your customer data now syncs seamlessly across all your devices — iPhone, iPad, and Android — through iCloud (on iOS) or your Google account (on Android). Edit a customer on one device and see the change everywhere within seconds.

What's improved:
• Edits made offline are preserved, even if another device syncs first
• Deleted customers stay deleted across all your devices (no more ghosts reappearing)
• Per-record sync is smarter — no all-or-nothing overwrites
• Service log entries and scheduled appointments now sync individually

Other fixes and polish:
• Square login is more reliable — tokens auto-refresh in the background so you stay connected longer
• Calendar events no longer occasionally drop on Android or shift by a day on iOS
• Faster customer search on lists with 500+ records
• Lots of behind-the-scenes hardening: tighter error messages, atomic cloud writes, smarter battery use during sync

If you ran into any sync glitches in the previous update, this fixes them.
