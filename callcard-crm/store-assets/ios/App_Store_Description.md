# App Store Description — Callout

> Max 4000 characters. Current count: ~2900.

---

**Callout keeps your customer base perfectly organized — one clean card per customer,
with the contact info and service history you need right at your fingertips.**

---

**BUILT FOR SERVICE PROFESSIONALS**

Whether you're a plumber, HVAC technician, electrician, landscaper, pest control
specialist, or any other trade professional who visits customers on a recurring schedule,
Callout is your go-to customer database. Keep every contact accurate and up to date —
no spreadsheet, no subscription, no clutter.

---

**KNOW WHO'S DUE FOR SERVICE**

Callout automatically tracks the last service date for every customer and lets you
know when their annual appointment is coming up — or overdue. Filter your customer list
by:

• Overdue — anyone past their 1-year mark
• 30 Days — service due in the next month
• 60 Days — plan two months ahead
• 90 Days — plan your full quarter

A badge on the Services tab keeps the count visible at all times, so you never lose
track of who needs attention.

---

**YOUR DATA, ONLY ON YOUR PHONE**

Every customer record, service log entry, and setting is stored locally on your device.
No account required. No cloud sync. No subscription fee. Your customer data never
leaves your phone unless you choose to share it.

---

**FEATURES**

• Customer database with name, email, phone, address, and zip code
• Service log per customer — date, type (Service or Install), and notes
• Annual service reminders with overdue badge + 30/60/90-day filter windows
• Search your customer list instantly by name, email, phone, address, or zip
• Sort customers by name (A–Z) or zip code
• Optional Square invoice integration — configure with your own API key to send
  invoices directly from a customer's card (coming soon)
