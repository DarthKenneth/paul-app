# Google Play Store Description — Callout

> Max 4000 characters. Full description shown on app detail page.

---

**Callout keeps your customer base perfectly organized — one clean card per customer,
with the contact info and service history you need right at your fingertips.**

---

**BUILT FOR SERVICE PROFESSIONALS**

Whether you're a plumber, HVAC technician, electrician, landscaper, pest control
specialist, or any other trade professional who visits customers on a recurring
schedule, Callout is your go-to customer database. Keep every contact accurate and
up to date — no spreadsheet, no subscription, no clutter.

---

**KNOW WHO'S DUE FOR SERVICE**

Callout automatically tracks the last service date for every customer and lets you
know when their annual appointment is coming up — or overdue. Filter your customer
list by:

✓ Overdue — anyone past their 1-year service mark
✓ 30 Days — service due in the next month
✓ 60 Days — plan two months ahead
✓ 90 Days — plan your full quarter

A badge on the Services tab keeps the count visible at all times, so you never lose
track of who needs attention.

---

**YOUR DATA, ONLY ON YOUR PHONE**

Every customer record, service log entry, and app setting is stored locally on your
device using Android's secure local storage. No account required. No cloud sync.
No subscription fee. Your customer data never leaves your phone unless you choose
to share it.

---

**FEATURES**

✓ Customer database — name, email, phone, address, zip code
✓ Service log per customer — date, type (Service or Install), notes; newest first
✓ Annual service reminders with overdue badge + 30/60/90-day filter windows
✓ Instant search by name, email, phone, address, or zip code
✓ Sort customers by name (A–Z) or zip code
✓ Optional Square invoice integration (configure with your own API key — coming soon)

---

**NO ACCOUNT. NO SUBSCRIPTION. NO TRACKING.**

We don't require an account, don't collect usage data, and don't have servers
storing your customer information. Everything lives on your device.

---

**COMING SOON**

• Square invoice integration — send invoices from a customer card in two taps
• Export and backup to CSV or cloud storage
• Customer photo support
