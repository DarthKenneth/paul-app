## Expected Rating

**IARC / ESRB Rating:** Everyone (E)
**Google Play Content Rating:** Rated for 3+

## Content Rating Justification

Rolodeck is a business productivity tool containing no user-generated content visible
to others, no violence, no sexual content, no gambling, and no controlled substance
references. All data entered by the user is stored locally and used solely for business
record-keeping purposes.
