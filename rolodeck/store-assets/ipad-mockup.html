Rolodeck — Phone vs. iPad Layout Mockup

Side-by-side of current iPhone layout vs. proposed iPad-optimized layout. v0.24.2 baseline.

What changes on iPad: bottom tab bar is replaced with a persistent left sidebar; the Customers list becomes a master-detail split view (list on the left, detail on the right, no drilling in/out); forms and cards cap their width and use multi-column layouts instead of stretching edge-to-edge; landscape orientation is unlocked.
Customers screen PRIMARY DIFFERENCE

Phone: tap a customer → push to detail screen → back button to return. iPad: list and detail are always visible. Selection updates the right pane instantly.
